A LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

MEET THE TEAM BEHIND TAI

TAI- The Aluminium Industry is not just a business; it's a legacy built on dedication, experience and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Our journey began in 1959 when it was founded by Mr. Srinivasan , and today, under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Gajendran a second-generation leader with over 40 years of experience, TAI continues to thrive.
